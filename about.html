Our Story

From One Truck to Utah County's Most Trusted Crew

Lehi Utah Tree Service was founded by Mike Jensen, a lifelong Lehi resident who spent years working for larger tree companies before deciding to start something better — a local business built on honest work, fair prices, and genuine care for the community he grew up in.

Starting with a single truck and a small crew of two, Mike built the business one satisfied customer at a time. Word spread quickly that this was a company that showed up on time, did clean work, and charged fair prices — and Lehi Utah Tree Service grew from there.

Today we're a team of 12 certified professionals serving all of Utah County, with an average Google rating of 4.9 stars across nearly 200 reviews.
