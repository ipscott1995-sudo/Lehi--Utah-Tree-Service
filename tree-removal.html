Expert Tree Removal for Lehi & Utah County Homeowners

Whether you have a dead oak in your backyard, a diseased cottonwood threatening your roof, or overgrown trees crowding your home, Lehi Utah Tree Service has the equipment and expertise to remove it safely — with zero damage to your property.

Our ISA-certified arborists evaluate every tree before removal to plan the safest approach. We use rigging, sectional cutting, and precision techniques that protect fences, landscaping, and structures even in tight spaces.

When Should You Remove a Tree?

  • The tree is dead or more than 50% diseased
  • Large dead branches threaten your roof or utility lines
  • The trunk shows signs of rot, cavities, or fungal growth
  • Storm damage has made the tree structurally unsound
  • Roots are damaging your foundation, driveway, or sewer lines
  • The tree is leaning dangerously toward structures or neighbors
  • You're building, landscaping, or reclaiming yard space

Our Tree Removal Process

Every job starts with a free on-site estimate. We assess the tree, discuss your concerns, and give you a written quote. On removal day, we:

  • Set up safety zones and protect surrounding landscaping
  • Section the tree top-down using professional climbing and rigging
  • Chip or haul away all branches and debris
  • Cut the stump to ground level (stump grinding available separately)
  • Leave your yard cleaner than we found it
